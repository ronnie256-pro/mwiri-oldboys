Privacy Policy

Mwiri Old Boys Association (MOBA)

Effective Date: 27 February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the official online portal of the Mwiri Old Boys Association (MOBA). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our alumni and members. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, register for an account, and engage with our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, House and cohort affiliation, contact details (email address, phone number, business address), and profession.

Financial Information: If you are a member of MOBA or use our payment services, we collect transaction details and your contribution history.

Engagement Data: Records of your stories, your images (captured during Mwiri events), subscriptions paid, donations made, and interactions with our digital content.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Community Building: Maintain an up-to-date alumni database.
  • Service Delivery: Process subscriptions and manage membership status.
  • Communication: Send relevant news, event invitations, and updates.
  • Administrative Records: Ensure compliance with governance and financial policies.
  • Security: Verify member identity and protect platform integrity.

4. How We Protect Your Data

  • Secure Storage: Financial and personal data are stored in secure, restricted-access databases.
  • Access Control: Only authorized Association officials (Admin only) have access.
  • Payment Security: We use reputable payment gateways. We do not store full credit card numbers or sensitive banking credentials.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only in the following contexts:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors operating under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: Where required by law, including compliance with the Computer Misuse Act of Uganda.
  • Internal Community: Within the secure members-only OB Connect directory (Mwirians only).

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectify: Update your profile information.
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of newsletters at any time.
  • Deletion: Request account removal, subject to governance and financial record requirements.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are directed at adult alumni of Busoga College Mwiri. We do not knowingly collect data from minors without appropriate oversight.
